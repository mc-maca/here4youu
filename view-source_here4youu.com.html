<p id="about-text"> Here4You (<a href="https://www.instagram.com/__here4you_" target="_blank">@__here4you_</a>) is a pro-choice, gender queer, shame free safe-space for
community and support for abortion, miscarriage, traumatic pregnancy, sexual trauma, and
endometriosis.<br><br>
The Here4You page is dedicated to exploring the intangible, contradictory, and sometimes
difficult feelings from experiences such as miscarriage, abortion, sexual assault, and chronic
pain through art and the sharing of stories.<br><br>
That we have been made to feel shameful and silenced about these experiences is not an
accident. We‚Äôve been taught to shove it down, to get over it, to keep on, and keep it to
ourselves. This is only to the benefit of those in power so we may not be inspired to come
together and fight back against the ongoing and immense siege on queer, trans, and birthing
bodies.<br><br>
The Here4You page is an attempt to unearth these experiences and feelings that have kept
many of us silent and riddled with shame, loneliness, and deep pain...
...through comics of flying, magical cats and virtual hugz.<br><br>
Here4You aspires to be the space that people need it to be. If you have any suggestions, or
