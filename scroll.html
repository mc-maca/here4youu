Here4You (@__here4you_) is a pro-choice, gender queer, shame free safe-space for community and support for abortion, miscarriage, traumatic pregnancy, sexual trauma, and endometriosis.

The Here4You page is dedicated to exploring the intangible, contradictory, and sometimes difficult feelings from experiences such as miscarriage, abortion, sexual assault, and chronic pain through art and the sharing of stories.

That we have been made to feel shameful and silenced about these experiences is not an accident. Weâ€™ve been taught to shove it down, to get over it, to keep on, and keep it to ourselves. This is only to the benefit of those in power so we may not be inspired to come together and fight back against the ongoing and immense siege on queer, trans, and birthing bodies.

The Here4You page is an attempt to unearth these experiences and feelings that have kept many of us silent and riddled with shame, loneliness, and deep pain... ...through comics of flying, magical cats and virtual hugz.

Here4You aspires to be the space that people need it to be. If you have any suggestions, or simply wish to contact, DM the @__here4you_ page on Instagram or email here4you333@gmail.com.
